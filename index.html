Why Study With Us

We provide diverse and relevant programs to meet the changing needs of ministry, where we offer a comprehensive curriculum that prepares students for meaningful service in a variety of fields.

Contextual and innovative learning which involves the application of Bible teachings to diverse ministry contexts. Our commitment to innovative teaching methods promotes engaging classroom experiences.

Our mission-driven vision is to fulfill Jesus' Great Commission by being a premier missional theological institution that prepares transformational leaders for the Church and society.

Our core values of Godliness and Character, Academic Excellence, Competent Leadership and Ministry, and Responsible Citizenship guide all aspects of our work. You shall be prepared academically and spiritually.

Our institution is affiliated with the Nigerian Baptist Convention (NBC) and overseen by its Ministerial Training Board, ensuring that our teachings are consistent with the principles of the Baptist faith.

We operate under a set of principles derived from the Scriptures, emphasizing the foundational beliefs that guide both individual conduct and institutional practices creating a conducive Christian environment.

Accreditations and Affiliation

All of our programs are affiliated with the Nigerian Baptist Convention (NBC), and accredited by the Baptist Accrediting Council for Theological Institutions in Nigeria (BACTSIN) and the Accrediting Council for Theological Education in Africa (ACTEA)

General Qualifications

  • Be at least twenty (20) years of age.
  • Provide satisfactory evidence of sound Christian character and a call of God to a Christian vocation.
  • Be recommended by his or her church and three (3) other recognized Christian workers.
  • Take and pass an entrance examination and an interview.
  • Candidates must have been a member of a Baptist church for at least two (2) years.
  • Applicants who are awaiting results at the time of application must submit a photocopy of their entry ticket for the examination, showing the date and type of examination. All results must be received by Baptist Theological Seminary, Kaduna before the applicant will be invited for the BTSK entrance examinations.
