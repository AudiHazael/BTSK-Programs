Study With Us

Welcome to the Baptist Theological Seminary Kaduna, where academic excellence meets spiritual transformation! As you begin your educational journey, allow us to highlight why choosing our institution aligns with your goals and values.

We provide diverse and relevant programs to meet the changing needs of ministry, where we offer a comprehensive curriculum that prepares students for meaningful service in a variety of fields.

We are accredited and approved, ensuring you receive high-quality education. All of our programs are accredited by the Baptist Accrediting Council for Theological Institutions in Nigeria (BACTSIN) and the Accrediting Council for Theological Education in Africa (ACTEA). This guarantees that you will receive a recognized and respected qualification upon completion.

Contextual and innovative learning involves applying Bible teachings to diverse ministry contexts. Our commitment to innovative teaching methods promotes engaging classroom experiences in both face-to-face and online settings.

Our mission-driven vision is to fulfill Jesus' Great Commission. Our vision is to be a premier missional theological institution that prepares transformational leaders for the Church and society. Your journey with us is about more than just academic achievement; it's about becoming a messenger of positive change.

Our core values of Godliness and Character, Academic Excellence, Competent Leadership and Ministry, and Responsible Citizenship guide all aspects of our work. The strategic objectives seek to prepare you not only academically but also spiritually, ensuring a holistic development that will equip you for leadership in a variety of capacities.

Our institution is affiliated with the Nigerian Baptist Convention (NBC) and overseen by its Ministerial Training Board. This connection ensures that our teachings are consistent with the principles of the Baptist faith, fostering a strong sense of community and shared purpose.

We operate under a set of principles derived from the Scriptures, emphasizing the foundational beliefs that guide both individual conduct and institutional practices. This creates a conducive Christian environment for learning and staying focused on the Seminary’s goals.

General Qualifications

  • Be at least twenty (20) years of age.
  • Provide satisfactory evidence of sound Christian character and a call of God to a Christian vocation.
  • Be recommended by his or her church and three (3) other recognized Christian workers.
  • Take and pass an entrance examination and an interview
  • In order to be admitted as a Baptist, the candidate must have been a member of a Baptist church for at least two (2) years.
  • Applicants who are awaiting results at the time of application must submit a photocopy of their entry ticket for the examination, showing the date and type of examination. All results must be received by Baptist Theological Seminary, Kaduna before the applicant will be invited for the BTSK entrance examinations.
