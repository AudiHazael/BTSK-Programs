Course Description

The Master of Divinity Theology is a three-year degree graduate programme incorporating courses in biblical studies, theological studies, religious educational studies, and practical theological studies. The purpose is to prepare biblically based (academically strong) pastors, missionaries, educators, worship leaders, and chaplains to address issues of Northern Nigeria and beyond. The programme is the foundational professional degree for ministry. It is designed to prepare students for the diverse ministries of leadership for graduate study in theology and related disciplines. The goal of the programme is to:

  • Prepare students to interpret the biblical writings in the Greek or Hebrew languages, using critical exegetical tools.
  • Provide a basic knowledge of the key movements and figures, and their significance in the history of Christianity.
  • Provide a basic knowledge of the contents and methods of Christian theology.
  • Equip students with the biblical, theological, and ethical undertanding necessary to analyze and discuss contemporary social issues affecting the church and society.
  • Provide opportunities to develop necessary skills for ministry within a variety of traditions, ministry settings, and cross-cultural contexts.
  • Enable students to develop and articulate a theology and theory of ministry that is relevant to their backgrounds and callings.
  • Promote a biblical/theological understanding of the person, work, and ministry of the Holy Spirit.

Requirements

The Master of Divinity is a three-year fully residential study. The maximum number of years of the programme is five years.

  • Candidates seeking admission into this programme must possess a minimum of a degree or its equivalent from a recognized institution of higher learning.
  • The applicant must have a minimum of second class lower classification (2.5) or better to be eligible.
  • Candidate without relevant first degree (especially Bachelor of Arts in CRS or equivalent) will be required to undertake one year Postgraduate Dipoma.
  • It is expected that the candidate had the minimum requirement as indicated in the general requirements.
