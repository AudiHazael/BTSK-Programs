Course Description

Women Institute is a centre for teaching and training of Christian women in order to develop each woman’s God given potential for service and leadership in her home, at church and in her community. The purpose of the Women Institute is stated as follows:

  • To enable pastors’ wives to work hand-in-hand with their husbands as a part of ministry team.
  • To develop women who will be capable and faithful servants of God through ministry to local churches.
  • To increase knowledge and skill levels of Christian women to empower them to be contributing members of their local communities and the society at large.

Requirements

Candidates for the Diploma in Theology and Diploma in Religious Education (which are three-year programmes) must fulfil all the general requirements. In addition, candidates seeking admission must have the following requirements:

  • He or she must have had theological training at a recognized institution.
  • He or she must be a church pastor or pastor’s wife.
  • He or she must be recommended by his or her Church, Association and Conference. If the candidate is not a Baptist, he or she must be recommended by his or her denominational head and two other recognized Christian workers within his or her denomination.
  • (Either) WASC with a minimum of four credits in relevant subjects, with English Language;
  • (Or) General Certificate of Education (Ordinary Level) with a minimum of four credits in relevant subjects, with English Language;
  • (Or) Senior Secondary School Certificate or NECO (Ordinary Level) with a minimum of four credits in relevant subjects, with English Language;
  • (Or) The Grade II Teacher’s Certificate with a minimum of four merit passes in relevant subjects and a merit pass in English Language; and,
  • (Or) NCE with four credits in relevant subjects with English Language.
  • It is expected that the candidate had the minimum requirement as indicated in the general requirements.
